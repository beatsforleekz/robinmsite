ROOTED IN HOUSE - BUILT WITH CULTURE

Nestled in the heart of London, ULU Records thrives as a boutique label passionate about musical exploration. Drawing from the city's diverse influences and culture, we've created a sanctuary where creativity flourishes and collaboration drives innovation.

The name 'ULU' is Hawaiian and has a double meaning: while being a breadfruit tree, it also means to grow, growth, rising and inspire. Reflecting this ethos, our logo features a leaf from the ULU tree — a tribute to our mission of supporting artists and helping their music grow.

At ULU, every beat tells a story. Our artists redefine sound, break boundaries, and shape the future of electronic music. We give them the freedom to experiment, evolve, and inspire.

Specialising in world-influenced house music, ULU Records blends deep, melodic tones with the emotive rhythms of Afro that stir the soul and ignite the spirit of adventure and clubs. Our music leaves a lasting imprint on hearts, minds and dance floors alike.
